Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you strictly provide to us when creating an account, subscribing to our newsletter, making a purchase, or communicating with our support team. The types of personal information we may collect include your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate our business and provide you with seamless service. We may also use this information for promotional communications and to present curated experiences on our site based on your interests.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your private data or personal information with outside entities, except for the purpose of fulfilling complex delivery orders, fraud prevention, or if required to comply with local and international laws.

4. Data Security

The security of your personal data is exceptionally important to us. We use premium security features to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal info.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please access our Contact Page and submit an inquiry.
