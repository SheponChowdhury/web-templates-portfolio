Frequently Asked Questions

What is your delivery time?

For ready-to-wear collections, global white-glove delivery takes 3-5 business days. For bespoke tailor-made items, the process requires 4-6 weeks for meticulous completion and fittings.

Do you offer returns?

Yes, we offer complimentary returns within 14 days of receipt for all unworn ready-to-wear items. Bespoke and made-to-measure garments are final sale due to their personalized nature.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major premium credit cards (Amex, Visa, Mastercard), Apple Pay, and secure bank wire transfers for high-value bespoke orders.

How can I track my order?

Once your order embarks with our luxury courier service, you will receive an exclusive tracking link via email and SMS text messaging, updating you at every transit milestone.
